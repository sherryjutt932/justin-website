About Flora

Flora is a project we've crafted for our esteemed client, an online platform that beautifully blends the essence of flowers with the convenience of a marketplace. ● Our goal was to provide families, couples, and individuals with a curated space for celebrating various occasions through stunning floral arrangements and bouquets. This project embodies the art of floral expression. We've meticulously designed Flora to offer a wide range of options, ensuring that each customer finds the perfect bouquet.

Our Approach

Our commitment to Flora's success is rooted in the invaluable insights gained from these stakeholder interviews. By understanding the distinct needs and aspirations of families, couples, and individuals seeking to celebrate various occasions with the beauty of flowers, ● we were able to create a platform that truly resonates with our customers. Flora, with its carefully curated floral marketplace, is the result of our dedication to translating these insights into a user-friendly and emotionally resonant online experience.

Structure

Wire Frames

After establishing a solid structure, we proceeded to create the wireframe. While keeping the specific concerns of each user group in focus, ● we went through multiple iterations of the wireframe, ultimately arriving at a user-friendly design. Our primary goal was to ensure that all user journeys flowed seamlessly and were user-friendly.
